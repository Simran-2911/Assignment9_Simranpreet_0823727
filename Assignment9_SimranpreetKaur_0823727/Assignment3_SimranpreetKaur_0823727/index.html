Gadar 2

Gadar: Ek Prem Katha is a 2001 Hindi-language romantic period action drama film directed by Anil Sharma based on a 1947 Indian Partition-era story by Shaktimaan Talwar. The movie, which is loosely based on the life of Boota Singh, stars Sunny Deol, Ameesha Patel, Amrish Puri, Lillete Dubey, and Vivek Shauq in the key parts. As a young actor, Utkarsh Sharma plays the role of Deol and Patel's son.Out of 500 girls who tried out for the part, Patel was chosen to play Sakeena.

Qismat 2

Jagdeep Sidhu is the author and director of the 2021 romantic drama film Qismat 2 in the Punjabi language. Shri Narotam Ji Productions and Zee Studios have provided financial support for the movie that Ankit Vijan and Navdeep Narula have produced. The movie, which also features Sargun Mehta and Ammy Virk, is a sequel to Qismat from 2018.

ATM

The story of the ATM web series is a game of survival for four buddies who are minor thieves caught up in a robbery case and wind up becoming the most sought-after suspects by the police. ATM is a criminal thriller with a focus on bank robberies that is set in the Musi River slums of Hyderabad.

Sunflower

Vikas Bahl is the creator of the ZEE5 original black comedy Sunflower. The series' co-director is Rahul Sengupta. Reliance Entertainment produced it. starring Sunil Grover as Sonu, an actor and comedian Ranvir Shorey, Girish Kulkarni, Mukul Chadda, Shonali Nagrani, Sonal Jha, and Ashish Vidyarthi are among the actors who appear in the cast.
